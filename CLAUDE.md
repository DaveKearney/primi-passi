# Primi Passi Play Therapy - Brand & Development Guide

## Brand Overview

**Brand Name:** Primi Passi Play Therapy
**Meaning:** "Primi Passi" is Italian for "First Steps" — representing the first steps a child takes toward emotional wellbeing through play.

---

## Visual Identity

### Logo Elements
- **Teddy bear** (sage green): Comfort, safety, childhood
- **Baby footprints** (lavender): Growth, first steps, gentle progress
- **Open book**: Learning, stories, understanding
- **Flower** (muted gold): Growth, nurturing, blossoming
- **Hearts & stars**: Warmth, wonder, care
- **Circular border** (lavender): Wholeness, safety, containment

### Color Palette
| Color | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| Lavender | `#C4A8D4` | Primary brand color, accents, borders |
| Sage Green | `#8DB48E` | Secondary color, icons, highlights |
| Muted Gold | `#D4C88A` | Tertiary accent, warmth elements |
| Soft Pink | `#E8D0D0` | Background tints, gentle accents |
| Warm Cream | `#FAF7F2` | Page backgrounds |
| Charcoal | `#4A4A4A` | Body text |

### Typography
- **Headings:** Rounded, warm serif or soft sans-serif (e.g., Nunito, Quicksand)
- **Body:** Clean, highly readable sans-serif (e.g., Nunito, Open Sans)
- **Style:** Friendly, approachable — never clinical or cold

---

## Brand Voice & Tone

### Voice Characteristics
- **Warm & Nurturing:** Like a trusted guide speaking to a caring parent
- **Reassuring:** Acknowledge parental concerns without judgment
- **Gently Professional:** Expert knowledge delivered in accessible, plain language
- **Empowering:** Help parents feel confident, not anxious
- **Hopeful:** Focus on growth and possibility, not problems

### Tone Guidelines
- Use "your child" not "the child" — personal and direct
- Avoid clinical jargon; explain concepts simply
- Lead with empathy, follow with information
- Use short paragraphs and clear structure — parents are busy
- Never be preachy or condescending
- Avoid fear-based messaging; focus on positive outcomes

### Writing Style
- Sentence case for headings (not ALL CAPS)
- Short sentences preferred
- Active voice
- Second person ("you", "your child")
- Contractions are welcome (it's, they're) for warmth

---

## Brand Personality

If Primi Passi were a person, they would be:
- A gentle, experienced professional who puts you at ease
- Someone who truly listens before offering guidance
- Warm but not overly familiar
- Knowledgeable without being intimidating
- The kind of person who makes you feel like everything will be okay

---

## Design Principles

1. **Softness over sharpness** — rounded corners, gentle gradients, no hard edges
2. **Breathing room** — generous whitespace, never cluttered
3. **Warmth over clinical** — feels like a nurturing space, not a hospital
4. **Accessibility first** — clear contrast, readable fonts, simple navigation
5. **Mobile-friendly** — parents browse on phones during nap time

---

## Technical Constraints

- Static HTML/CSS/JS hosted on GitHub Pages
- No backend, no database, no localStorage
- Single-page website
- All assets inline or CDN-hosted
- Responsive design (mobile-first)

---

## Content Pillars

1. **What is Play Therapy** — demystifying the process
2. **How it helps** — concrete benefits parents can expect
3. **What to expect** — practical session information
4. **Who it's for** — helping parents identify if it's right for their child
5. **About / Contact** — building trust and connection
